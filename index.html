What is Included in a Private Desert Safari Package?

A private desert safari offers an exclusive and personalized experience in the stunning landscapes of the Arabian desert. Unlike standard tours, a private safari allows you to enjoy the beauty and thrill of the desert with just your group, ensuring a more intimate and tailored adventure.

In this article, we will explore what is typically included in a private desert safari package, helping you understand why this experience is a must when visiting Dubai.

What Cultural Experiences Can I Expect from Arabian Tour Packages?

Key Inclusions in a Private Desert Safari Package

1. Transportation

  • Pick-Up and Drop-Off: Most private desert safari packages include complimentary pick-up and drop-off services from your hotel or residence in a comfortable 4x4 vehicle. This ensures a hassle-free start and end to your adventure.

2. Dune Bashing

  • Thrilling Dune Drive: Experience the adrenaline rush of dune bashing, where skilled drivers navigate through the towering sand dunes. This exhilarating ride typically lasts around 20-30 minutes, offering breathtaking views of the desert landscape.

3. Camel Riding

  • Traditional Camel Ride: Enjoy a serene camel ride, often lasting about 20 minutes. This traditional mode of transport provides a unique perspective of the desert and connects you with local Bedouin culture.

4. Sandboarding

  • Exciting Sandboarding Experience: For those looking for more adventure, many packages include sandboarding on the dunes. This thrilling activity allows you to glide down sandy slopes, adding an extra layer of excitement to your safari.

5. Cultural Experiences

  • Henna Painting: Engage in the traditional art of henna painting, where skilled artists adorn your hands with intricate designs.
  • Shisha Smoking: Relax and enjoy shisha (water pipe) in a comfortable setting, immersing yourself in local customs.

6. Dining Options

  • BBQ Dinner: A highlight of any private desert safari is the BBQ dinner served at a Bedouin-style camp. Enjoy a feast featuring both vegetarian and non-vegetarian options under the stars, often accompanied by live entertainment.

7. Entertainment

  • Cultural Performances: Experience captivating performances such as belly dancing and Tanoura dance shows that showcase local traditions and artistry.

8. Refreshments

  • Unlimited Drinks: Most packages include unlimited refreshments like Arabic coffee, tea, soft drinks, and water throughout your experience to keep you hydrated in the desert heat.

Conclusion

A private desert safari is an unforgettable way to explore the enchanting landscapes of the Arabian desert while indulging in thrilling activities and rich cultural experiences. With inclusions like dune bashing, camel rides, sandboarding, and delightful dining options, every moment is designed to create lasting memories. Whether you're traveling with family or friends, consider booking a private desert safari for an exclusive adventure that truly captures the essence of this magnificent region. Prepare for an extraordinary journey that combines excitement with cultural immersion!
